About us

I Saw Bunnies in 2017 is a non-profit organization* founded to attest the fact that I did see bunnies in 2017. 2017 was not the only time I've seen bunnies in my life, and I've seen them since, but I happen to have pictures of these bunnies (see the "Photos" page for more details).

In case you were wondering why there is Japanese text, it's just to practice using it in HTML and CSS.

*Please note that 'I Saw Bunnies in 2017' is not actually an organization.
